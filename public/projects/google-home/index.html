Google Home Redesign

Overview

I art directed a mock redesign of the Google Home app, with a focus on simplifying the user experience. I chose the app because I found it overwhelming and inaccessible.

Key changes included eliminating the initial screen, introducing an introductory setup popup, emphasizing home names, and transforming the TV control bar into a widget. Additionally, I restructured the "Devices" and "Settings" sections for improved readability, organization, and visual appeal.
